Who we are ?

SharpIdea is a software development company. Our experienced team of specialists develops advanced mobile and web products. We approach each project as an opportunity to use our experience, creativity, intuition to deliver results that often exceed expectations. Our mission is to help people develop their dream products. We need to understand the goals of a project to select the best growth opportunities and technology solutions. We love finding new and efficient ways to work.

Why Choose SHARPIDEA as Your Software Development Company

Professionalism

A blend of traditional approach and modern solutions are married to three decades of experience here, at SharpIdea. SharpIdea Software business analysts, system architects, QA engineers and senior level programmers for hire available for you, project by project approach and technical intelligence are our differentiating factors.

Responsibility

Our professionalism is tightly intertwined with the high level of responsibility when delivering projects. This creates the atmosphere of accurate estimates, deadlines compliance, and responsiveness to business needs. Our clients are welcome to control every stage of software development, including face-to-face meetings with designers or back-end programmers upon demand.

Transparency

The client-oriented approach is the core of our corporate culture, its main rule stating: be transparent and flexible to find the most suitable solution for unique requests of our clients. The win-win strategy, we adhere to, allows our clients to make the best choice for their business and on their own terms.
